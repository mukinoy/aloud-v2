WHERE ALOUD COMES IN

90% of the visually impaired have partial visual empairment. Depending on the severity and cause, this leaves a wide range of ways to read.

Instead of depending of the hardly provided Braille or rely on the release of a books’ Audiobooks, Aloud improves the experience of reading physical books for the people within this spectrum.
